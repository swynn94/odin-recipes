Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a common pasta dish typically covered in a tomato sauce.

Commonly it is served with meatballs and topped with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients for my Spaghetti

Spaghetti
  • 1 lb of Dry Linguine
  • 1 16 oz Jar of Tomato Sauce
  • 5-8 Basil Leaves
  • Parmesan to Taste
  • Salt (use in water during boiling)
  • Olive Oil (as needed)
Meatballs
  • 1 Lb of 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 1 Cup Breadcrumbs
  • 1 White Onion
  • 1/2 Cup Parsley
  • Crushed Red Pepper to Taste
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps for my Spaghetti

  1. Preheat skillet (meatballs) and bring pot of water to boil (spaghetti).
  2. Combine all ingredients in meatballs together.
  3. pour olive oil in pan and sear all sides of meatballs.
  4. Pour jar of tomato sauce into pan with meatballs.
  5. Place pasta into boiling water until tender.
  6. Drain pasta and pour into skillet with meatballs and sauce to combine.
  7. Plate entire dish and top with basil and parmesan. Enjoy!
