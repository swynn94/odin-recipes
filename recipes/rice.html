Cilantro and Lime Rice

Description

Cilantro and lime rice is a rice dish. Famously made at Chipotle.

Ingredients for my Cilantro and Lime Rice

  • 2.5 Cups White Rice
  • 2 Limes
  • 1 Lemon
  • 1 Bunch Cilantro
  • 2.5 Tbsp Butter
  • 2.5 Cups Water
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps for my Cilantro and Lime Rice

  1. Rinse rice in cold water until water runs clear.
  2. Place rice and water into pressure cooker for 4 mins.
  3. Chop cilantro finely then zest lemons and limes.
  4. Once pressure cooking has dropped in pressure, remove lid.
  5. Add butter, cilantro, and zest to rice.
  6. Juice each of the lemons and limes completely over rice.
  7. Add salt and pepper then stir/flugg rice to combine.
  8. Plate and enjoy.
