Cheeseburger

Description

A cheeseburger is typically a ground beef patty topped with cheese between two buns.

Most oftenly the cheeseburger is accompanied by various condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, etc.) and toppings (tomatoes, pickles, onion, lettuce and more).

Ingredients for my Cheeseburger

  • 1/4 lb of 80/20 Ground Beef
  • 1 Slice of American Cheese
  • 1 Sesame Seed Bun
  • 1 Medium Sized Leaf of Lettuce
  • 1 Tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 Tbsp Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps for my Cheeseburger

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat on stovetop.
  2. Form groundbeef into patty shape and spray skillet with cooking spray.
  3. Place patty down onto skillet and press firmly. Salt and pepper side facing upwards.
  4. After roughly 5 minutes, flip patty and add salt and pepper to side now facing up.
  5. Place buns in toaster and add cheese to patty to melt.
  6. Once buns are toasted, turn off heat on stovetop but leave skillet on burner.
  7. Apply condiments and place lettuce onto toasted buns.
  8. Remove burger patty from skillet and place atop lettuce on bun.
  9. Top your burger with top bun and enjoy.
